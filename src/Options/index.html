Services

You have no active Services. SyncDex won't do anything until you add one.

There is enabled but inactive services. Click the Reload or Login button to fix them.

Colors

Colors that exist in HTML are accepted but you can also use values like #RRGGBB, rgb(red, green, blue) or with alpha channel rgba(red, green, blue, alpha).

If you want to disable one of the highlights you can turn it off globally or set a color to transparent.

Import

Importing will only update your SyncDex save and is used to initialize SyncDex.

If you wish to update any external Service, see Export

Export

Exporting will update your Online save on the Service you choose using your SyncDex save.

You should Import from somewhere before exporting to somewhere else !

Delete

Click the Delete button twice to permanently erase your save.

It can't be restored, Export first if you want to keep a copy.
