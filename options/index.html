Colors

Colors that exist in HTML are accepted but you can also use values like #RRGGBB, rgb(red, green, blue) or with alpha channel rgba(red, green, blue, alpha).
If you want to disable one of the highlights you can turn it off globally or set a color to transparent.

Highlight

Paint rows in chapters lists to quickly identify which chapters you can read next or that you already read.

Next Chapter

Color of the next chapter of a title.

Higher Chapters

Color of all higher chapters than your last read chapter.

Lower Chapters

Color of all lower chapters than your last read chapter.

Current Chapters

Colors for all current chapters.
SyncDex will cycle through them if you add multiple of them.

The following option is only applied on a title page.

Opened Chapters

Colors for chapters that you opened if the "Save opened chapters" option is enabled.

Updates

The following options change the behavior while you are reading.

Save opened chapters

Keep a list of all opened chapters when read.

Save only next chapter

Save the chapter you are reading only if it's the next one.
If "Confirm Chapter save" is enabled it will asks you if you still want to update it, or else it will do nothing.

Save only higher chapter

Save the chapter you are reading only if it's higher.
If "Confirm Chapter save" is enabled it will asks you if you still want to update it, or else it will do nothing.

Confirm Chapter

Asks if you want to update your list(s) to the current chapter you are reading.

Update only if in list

Update your list(s) only if the title you are reading is already in your MangaDex list.

Lists

Hide Higher chapters

Hide chapters that are higher than the last chapter you read.

Hide Lower chapters

Hide chapters that are lower> than the last chapter you read.

Hide Last chapters

Hide the last chapter you read.

Add covers

Display the cover of the title when hovering a row.

Original cover

Display the full size original cover of the title instead of a thumbnail.
Do not enable this if you don't have a somehow good connection, some images can be big.

Check Service status

Check the status of a title on a Service.
This incrase the amount of requests to Services and is not recommended.
It is actually useless if you only read on MangaDex through the site and with SyncDex enable.
This option can solve problems if you read and update your list through another application like Tachiyomi on mobile and your SyncDex local status isn't up to date.
There is an hard limit to the amount of requests that can be made and SyncDex will only try to get the status of titles that could have changed, titles with chapters updated more than 15 minutes ago and that haven't been already checked in the past hour.
